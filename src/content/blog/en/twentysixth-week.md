## Week 26 Reflection

While continuing my daily **algorithm studies** and **English studies**, there were two major developments this week:

  As new classes for the semester began in earnest, I ensured I secured enough time to keep up with my academic work and strove to balance both. I am practicing flexible time management in response to the change in environment.
- **Start of Job Hunting**
  I have finally begun to focus seriously on job hunting toward my future goals. I am taking concrete actions to leverage my learning outcomes and development experience and connect them to the next step in my career.

## Week 27 Goals
