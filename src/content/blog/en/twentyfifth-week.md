# The Start of a New Semester and Learning Management

Hello, this is Haruta Watanabe.

First, I apologize for the delay in this post.

While I usually develop mobile apps and websites, I have now completed the 25th week of my studies with a view towards a career as a software engineer and data scientist. This week was an important one as the new university semester began, prompting me to review my daily rhythm and learning plan.

## Week 25 Reflection

This week, I continued to prioritize **algorithm studies** while progressing with **English studies** in parallel.

Also, university classes finally resumed this week. With the start of new academic courses, it became an opportunity for me to think deeply again about **time allocation and learning management**—specifically, how to balance university academics and engineering self-study at a high level. I will be implementing strategies to make the most of my limited time.

## Week 26 Goals

In week 26, while continuing to solidify my computer science foundation, I will work on elevating my English to a more practical skill level.

- **Continued Study of Algorithms and Data Structures**
  I will continue to deepen my understanding of both fields, which are at the core of efficient program design.
- **Focus on Practical English Learning (Listening & Speaking)**
  Aiming for English proficiency that can be used in actual communication rather than just for input, I will focus on listening and speaking training.
